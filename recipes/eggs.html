Scrambled Eggs

Description

The secret to the best scramble? Mayonnaise and butter, a good whisk, and a nonstick pan over low heat. I love scrambled eggs.

The right way to do eggs. There is more to it than just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook.
  4. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist. Do not over cook.
